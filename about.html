How my story begins...

During the bleak, isolated times of the Covid pandemic whilst locked away in my bedroom in Tottenham, North London finishing a Masters in Applied Theatre, an app idea for connecting creative people suddenly came to me. With a background in drama education and teaching, I knew nothing about the tech world at all; but my project spurred me on to educate myself.

She Codes...

After presenting a portfolio of my idea to a developer to get a quote, I decided that I had learnt a lot. After a decade in education, I'd been enthused by this whole new world of tech. I wanted to be in a position to build my own ideas. Towards the end of 2022 having moved back to Barcelona, I stumbled upon a coding course that I was confident would send me on my way- She Codes.
